About the System

A state-of-the-art automated tabulation solution developed by the Junior Philippine Computer Society - CSPC Chapter.

The Mission

Precision & Efficiency

The Quiz Bee Tabulation System was architected to eliminate manual computation errors and provide real-time, transparent results for academic competitions.

Built with advanced algorithms to handle Hybrid Scoring (Cumulative + Clean Slate Final) and Iterative Tie-Breaking, this system ensures that every winner is determined fairly and accurately.

Hybrid Scoring

Seamlessly switches between cumulative points for elimination rounds and back-to-zero scoring for the finals.

Smart Tie-Breaker

Automatically detects ties at qualifying cutoffs and generates sudden-death clincher rounds instantly.

Official Reports

Generates signed, secured, and formatted PDF result sheets ready for printing and archiving.
